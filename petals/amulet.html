Air

"It's literally nothing."

What Does Air Do?

Air, when equipped in the game, increases your flower radius size and behaves as if you have one less petal slot. Bubbles almost always drop it.

Air does not do any damage but it makes your hitbox bigger which may be used to have a bigger petal collection radius or maybe you can use it to attack a ultra or super termite overmind as you can switch petals out with the air.
