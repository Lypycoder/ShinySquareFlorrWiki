What is Florr.io?

Florr.io is a game where you are a flower and you fight enemies which are mostly bugs(not the computer type of bug), you start with 5 basics that do 5 dmg and you can upgrade your health, petal rotation speed etc.

You can kill mobs to get xp and petals(which you attack with) and you can upgrade talents using talent points(which you get from leveling up with xp), also leading to crafting many rarities of petals. The rarity of petals are: common, unusual, rare, epic, legendary, mythic, ultra, super (which are really hard to get, most people who have super petals are level 120 or above). I currently have mythic petals since my account got reset for some reason in Feb 2024(I had way better petals)
